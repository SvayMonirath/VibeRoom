Our Story
Luna was born from a simple idea: music sounds better when shared. We noticed that despite having access to millions of songs, people were missing the communal experience of enjoying music together.

In 2025, we set out to create a platform that would bridge the gap between solo streaming and social connection. Today, VibeRooms has grown into a thriving community where music lovers from around the world gather to share their favorite tracks, discover new artists, and make lasting friendships.

Whether you're studying late at night with friends across continents, hosting a virtual dance party, or simply chilling to lo-fi beats with strangers who become friends, VibeRooms provides the perfect space for these magical musical moments.
